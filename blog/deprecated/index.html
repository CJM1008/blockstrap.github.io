why we deprecated our public api

There are lots of APIs in the ecosystem, so after much thought, we have concluded that we can bring the most value to the community by re-focusing the blockstrap brand to specifically become just a modular blockchain agnostic HTML wallet framework that supports multiple APIs, themes and plugins...

Can't wait to see what you'll build next!
