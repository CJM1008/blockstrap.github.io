we're switching off on 27th november

After much thought and an incredible time working with developers, startups, accelerators, and friends from across the world, we’ve come to the difficult decision of deprecating the Blockstrap API on November 27th 2015. In the meantime, and until long after, we will continue to support the development of our framework, as we also continue to explore other blockchain-related endeavours that we will soon share with you. Blockstrap is a product of its time - and that time is ’not yet’. Thank you for the wonderful support.
Mark Smalley Neuroware.io CEO
